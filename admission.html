Our Admission Procedure

Admission to various classes Creche, Prenursery, Nursery 1&2, JSS, SSS Primary 1-6 section is open to students throughout the year.

Admission forms and prospectus can be obtained from the school at a token fee.

The forms should be duly completed and submitted to the School Admissions Officer. General Entrance Examinations are usually conducted at the end of the third term for students intending to get admitted into all class sections in the School.

We are known for our quality and excellence; this is why we are ranked among the best primary school in Lagos. Our secondary school are rated as one of the best secondary school in Lagos.
