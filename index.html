Brief History of luz School

Established in January 2016 by diving inspiration and driven by pure gusto for excellence. Luz School radiates the modern-day best standards in both learning and character development of children, providing high academic, moral, and social content that makes her pupils and students stand out in any situation and place, thereby preparing them to conquer the world in their desired and well nurtured chosen academic path, making Luz the best among peers.

Our school programme is a hybrid of the best curriculums available at all times, thus we engage in a regular review of our programmes to remain at the zenith of academic excellence for our children.

Our purpose is to produce children who are strong-willed in the excellence of academics, morals, and social character to be able to remain shining lights in any part of the world after Luz.
